Method

RFM from fact_order (delivered) → log1p(F/M) + RobustScaler → K-Means (k by max silhouette).

Why RFM first?

Unsupervised, low leak risk, strong business labels — ideal first ML case study on the warehouse.

Next (phase 2)

Late-delivery classification (supervised) using order / geo / seller features without post-delivery leakage.

Why 4 facts?

Different grains (item / order / payment / review) avoid fan-out that would inflate GMV or double-count delivery SLA.

Customer key

customer_unique_id models the real shopper; customer_id is order-scoped and stays in the bridge map only.
